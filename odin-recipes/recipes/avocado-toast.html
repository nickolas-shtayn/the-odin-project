Avocado Toast

This vibrant toast takes classic avocado toast to a whole new level! Creamy avocado gets a fiery kick from homemade chili oil, while a perfectly soft-boiled egg adds a luxurious touch. Fresh microgreens and a sprinkle of sesame seeds bring a burst of flavor and texture in every bite

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of high-quality bread (sourdough, whole wheat, or your favorite)
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon homemade chili oil (see previous recipe!)
  • 1 soft boiled egg (see previous recipe!)
  • Pinch of Maldon flaky salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup microgreens (such as pea shoots or sunflower greens)
  • 1 tablespoon white and black sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Toast your bread to your desired level of crispness. You can use a toaster, a pan with a little butter or oil, or a grill.
  2. While the bread toasts, mash the avocado in a bowl. Season it with a pinch of salt and pepper for extra flavor.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread. Drizzle with a generous tablespoon of your homemade chili oil, letting it pool a little for a visually stunning presentation.
  4. Carefully peel your soft-boiled egg and place it on top of the avocado toast. Season the egg with a little more salt and pepper, if desired.
  5. Garnish your toast with a sprinkle of microgreens for a fresh, vibrant touch.
  6. Top it all off with sesame seeds for a nutty flavor and a textural contrast.
